The Mid-Week Motor Strategy

This section outlines the core reasoning behind establishing a monthly Bike Night. The goal is to transform slower Tuesday nights into a high-revenue community pillar by engaging Monroe's dense, high-visibility motorcycle culture.

The Core Hooks

  • 1.
    Incentive: Half-Price Appetizers Already approved by GM. Acts as the primary culinary draw to get riders in seats.
  • 2.
    Safety Angle: "Rider Fuel" To ensure a safe event, we push a $5 bottomless non-alcoholic drink special (e.g., Red Bull slushies, craft sodas) with an app purchase. Keeps the vibe responsible.
  • 3.
    The Hero Charity: Operation Rally Point Based locally in Monroe on N Midland. Focusing on veteran support aligns perfectly with the target demographic.
  • 4.
    The Vibe: Low-Speed, High-Engagement Features a "Bike of the Night" award and a skill-based "Slow Race" to keep guests engaged in the lot before/after eating.

Regulatory & Safety Constraints

Ensuring a frictionless event means adhering strictly to local ordinances and state laws regarding charity collections. Review these guardrails.

Donation Mechanics (GA Law)

WARNING: Standard raffles (selling tickets for a prize) are heavily regulated in Georgia and require a complex Sheriff's permit for the 501c3.

Recommended Legal Alternatives:

  • ✅
    "Pass the Boot"

    Physical, voluntary cash collection by Operation Rally Point reps at a designated table. Simplest execution.

  • ✅
    Percentage Night

    Applebee's pledges a flat 10% of all sales generated between 6:00 PM and 9:00 PM directly to the charity.

🔊 Noise Ordinances

Monroe restricts "Electronic Amplification" visible from the roadway.

Directive: No live outdoor bands without a specific municipal permit. Music must remain at "patio background" levels, and must be strictly controlled after 9:00 PM.

🌧️ Weather & Safety Policy

Rain Date: If precipitation probability exceeds 40% at 2:00 PM on event day, the event automatically pushes to the following Tuesday. All marketing must state this clearly.

Beverage Control: Servers instructed to actively push the $5 "Rider Fuel" N/A specials to motorcycle operators.
